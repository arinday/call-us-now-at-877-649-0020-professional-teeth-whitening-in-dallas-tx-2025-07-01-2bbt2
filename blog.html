The Complete Guide to Professional Teeth Whitening: What to Expect

Professional teeth whitening is a controlled dental procedure using high-concentration bleaching agents to remove surface stains and deep discoloration. Unlike over-the-counter alternatives, this treatment is customized and supervised by dental experts, offering faster, more dramatic, and longer-lasting results. The 60-90 minute procedure includes consultation, cleaning, gum protection, gel application, and possible light activation. Results can last 6 months to 2 years with proper maintenance. While costs vary, the investment provides professional oversight and reliable outcomes. Important considerations include existing dental work, sensitivity, and gum health. Regular touch-ups help maintain that bright, confident smile indefinitely.
